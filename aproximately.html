ALEXANDRA CRETU

I have been collecting sugar bags for 2 years and 5 months. The work ~approximately contains this collection almost entirely and is the confession of some little personal obsessions. Some of them have to do with going out for coffee, a custom that is specific to the collective daily routine, and with the calculation of the calories consumed (or not) from the sugar used for coffee.
Other ones have to do with the need to document everything, in order not to forget.
The jars labeled by personal criteria and the notebook with the selection of sugar bags are the result of the subjective documentation of this ongoing collection.
The diagram and the schemes are an attempt of objective auto analysis, through a deliberate rigidity.
